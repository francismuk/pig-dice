Pig Dice Game

Welcome to the most amazing pig dice game. Enjoy playing

Rules

Rules

Here are the rules for playing this game

  • Roll on every turn
  • All your points will be recorded
  • Roll 1 or more time until you want to
  • if you get a one in any of your rolls you get a 0 for that round

Instructions

How to play

  • Ensure that you know the objectives of this game
  • Read through the rules on the link provided
  • Roll the dice by pressing the roll button
  • Roll again if you want to(You may roll as many times as you want if you feel you are lucky)
  • When you are done the opponent does the same thing
  • Always make sure that you are having fun

Objectives

Objectives

  • The main objective is to score 100 points before your opponent
  • Have fun!
